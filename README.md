##Date Palm Farmer Assistant (DateCare) 
# Introduction 
1 Background Information 
Date palm farming is one of the most important agricultural activities in Oman and many Middle Eastern countries. Farmers often face challenges such as tracking harvest seasons, identifying diseases, monitoring market prices, and maintaining proper farm records. 
Many farmers still depend on traditional methods, which may reduce productivity and cause financial losses due to the lack of organized and timely information. With the growth of mobile technology, digital solutions can help farmers improve farm management and decision-making. 
DateCare is developed as a smart mobile application to support date palm farmers through simple and effective digital tools. 
1.2 Project Objectives 
To help farmers track harvest schedules efficiently.  
To provide information about common date palm diseases and treatments.  
To display market prices of dates for better selling decisions.  
To allow farmers to record and manage farm notes digitally.  
To improve productivity and farm management using mobile technology. 
# Scope 
Included: 
Harvest calendar  
Disease identification guide  
Market price tracking  
Farm notes management
